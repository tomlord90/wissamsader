cardinal points

2019 an ongoing collaboration project with farah hazim

cardinal points is a time based collaborative project with sound artist farah hazim, it tacles the way we understand the reality of media and interconnectivity. While present in the performance, we appear only as operators, conductors, observers.

Superposing and extracting different live streamed events (from radios to phone calls..), all concentrated in one space. A temporary media space, in which things accumulate and dissipate. Desireful of building temporary links and opening spaces for aesthetic interaction between different territories and geographical regions in a complexity of « surimposed territories and interwined histories » (E. Saïd, 1977)

Live at Studium P in vilnius, lithuania February 2020

this project is on ongoing developements. Format is constantly evolving through experimentations and new ideas.
