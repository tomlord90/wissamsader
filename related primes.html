Related Primes

Realized at KW Institute for Contemporary Art on the 13-15th April 2017.

Working with Berlin-based visual artist Dafna Maimon and Emma Howes over an eight-week period with a cast of 10 artists for the performance Related Primates, (2017)

Related Primates was a 3-hour long non-verbal “directed experience” that engaged the audience with performative vignettes, participatory exercises, sculptural interventions and sensory stimulations using, edibles, sound and movement.
