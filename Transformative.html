Abolitionist Jelly Jam

Transforming Connections Within Dissonance. The Jam is organized by the research group currently working under the name, Abolitionist Jelly formed in July 2023 in Berlin. We are artists, musicians, cultural workers, writers and researchers and focus on abolitionism and transformative justice practices. Our group meets twice a month to share texts, podcasts, videos, ideas, and experiences to grow, learn, question, explore, grieve, vent, cry, and celebrate together with no specified outcomes.
